Rules and Regulations

Follow this:

  • Always bring your valid ID when borrowing of books

  • Always bring alcohol and wear facemask inside the library

  • Each client may only borrow a maximum of three to five (3-5) book at a time

  • Silence inside the library must be observed at all times

  • Smoking, eating, and talking loudly are stricly prohibited in the library

  • You can bring your own laptop, iPad, cellphones and other gadgets inside the library
